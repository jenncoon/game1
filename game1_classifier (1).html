AI is Reliable Here AI performs consistently on large-scale processing, pattern recognition, translation, and summarization. Speed and consistency are genuine strengths when accuracy can be verified after the fact.
AI Often Misses This Tasks requiring nuanced reading of tone, relationship context, or lived experience tend to trip AI up. It may produce a plausible-sounding response that misses what a human would catch immediately.
Human Judgment Adds Most Value Ethical decisions, emotional attunement, and high-stakes interpersonal situations benefit most from human presence. The cost of getting these wrong often outweighs any efficiency gain from delegation.
Context Determines the Best Tool Some tasks work well with AI in low-stakes or first-draft situations but need human review before they matter. The same task can belong here or in another category entirely depending on how it is used.
